About the Cipher

The Caesar Cipher is one of the most basic encryption methods. It works by shifting letters in the alphabet by a certain number.

Julius Caesar used this cipher to send secret military messages. Even though it is not secure today, it helps understand how encryption started.

This tool only works with letters. Numbers and symbols will stay the same.
